Shapes of Play2020

The Shapes of Play project, a 1:1 group exhibition as part of Dutch Design Week 2020, which showcased performative and expressive playground elements created in collaboration with design studio Ossidiana and terrazzo manufacturer Tomaello. 

The playground aims to unleash the imagination of what playgrounds can be and transform it into an experimental public space for all age groups.

Materials: concrete, wood, shells, porcelain, stones, ytong block
