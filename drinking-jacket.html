Drinking Jacket2023

Join Sasha on her night journey! Sasha loves to absorb alcohol during the night adventures, and she’s wearing a suitable garment – a drinking jacket!
The jacket provides pockets for bottles, an emergency cigarette, and most importantly- a comfortable and warm seat, so she can continue to drink outside during the winter without having a urinal infection.

The Drinking Jacket addresses the ongoing heavy alcohol usage between young-generation in the Baltics. Instead of forbidding to sell of alcohol at numerous events and trying to restrict alcohol selling more and more, the jacket is a helpful and absurd gesture to have warm clothing and provide a less-harmful environment for drinking. On the back you’ll read the embroidered sentence: If I’m not gonna get drunk on Friday, I will definitely get drunk on Monday
